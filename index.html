Virtual Hangout

Opening on 25th October 2023

Virtual Hangout is a brand-new concept coming soon to Glasgow City Centre. We have 15 zones where you can ‘Virtually’ play your favourite game in first player mode. We use the very best VR Headsets so you are fully immersed in the experience.

The ‘Group Zone’ accommodates 4 players where you can join Virtual Worlds or play against each other in some of the newest games available.
